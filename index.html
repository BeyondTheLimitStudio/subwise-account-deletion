Account and Data Deletion Request

At SubWise ("Beyond The Limit Studio"), we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data.

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your SubWise account and all associated data connected to it at any time. When you choose to delete your account, we will permanently wipe all cloud-synced subscriptions, premium status records, and profile associations from our databases.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your data is deleted, we cannot recover your subscription history or previous syncs.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

You can instantly delete all of your data directly from within the SubWise app. This is the fastest and most secure method.

  1. Open the SubWise Android app.
  2. Tap on the Profile tab (or go to Settings).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the warning and tap Delete Forever to confirm.

Your user profile and all cloud-stored subscriptions will be immediately erased, and you will be signed out naturally.

How to Request Data Deletion (Via Email)

If you no longer have access to the app and wish to have your account deleted, you can request manual deletion via email.

  1. Send an email to help.beyondthelimitstudio@gmail.com from the Google Account address you used to sign into SubWise.
  2. Use the subject line: "SubWise Account Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, state clearly that you want your account and all associated data permanently removed.

Note: For security reasons, we can only process your request if you email us from the exact address linked to your SubWise account. We will process these requests within 7 to 14 business days.

What Data Is Deleted?

Upon confirmation of deletion (either in-app or via email), the following information is permanently destroyed from our Cloudflare D1 servers:

  • Your user record (associated Google Email address).
  • All your tracked subscriptions, their costs, renewal dates, and metadata.
  • Any active Premium Status flags tied to your account.

Since we do not store sensitive payment information (purchases are handled securely by Google Play Billing), there is no payment data to delete on our end.
